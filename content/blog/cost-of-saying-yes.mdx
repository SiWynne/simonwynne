Every yes is a no to something else — you just don't always see the other
side of that trade in the moment.

## Time is the real currency

Saying yes to a favour, a meeting, a project — none of it is free. It comes
out of the same finite pool as everything else you care about.

## A useful filter

Before agreeing to something, ask what you'd have to say no to in order to
do it well. If the honest answer is "the thing that actually matters to me,"
that's usually the answer you need.
