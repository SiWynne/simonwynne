A brand isn't a logo or a colour palette. It's the sum of every small promise
you've kept, multiplied by how many people noticed.

## Consistency beats intensity

A single impressive project won't build a lasting reputation. Ten
unremarkable-but-reliable ones will. People trust patterns, not peaks.

## The details compound

The way you reply to an email, the way you handle a missed deadline, the way
you hand off work — none of these show up on a case study, and all of them
decide whether someone works with you again.

Treat the small stuff like it's the whole job, because eventually it is.
